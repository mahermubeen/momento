Borsalino
Eyewear

WEBSITE | SOCIAL MEDIA

Borsalino is an eyewear company from Argentina that was looking to boost sales and increase brand awareness. We designed a minimalistic web site that respects user´s time by showing the full catalog in a single page, makes the products the main protagonists and allows the campaign photos to set the visual tone. With this new approach and the addition of a geolocation-based store locator the conversion rate went up by +200%.

  • Social Media

    Right from the start we knew that both the web site and the social media channels had to work in tandem to achieve the goals. That´s why we created a cohesive design language to apply visual consistency across all the digital platforms and convey the type of trust that helps users to make a buying decision.

    Also, we are in charge of all the strategy and create the content (including photography). Through a combination of organic reach, paid ads, and deals with influencers, the traffic to the website increased 80% and Borsalino reaches an audience almost 8 times larger than before for a fraction of the money that was investing in advertisement.
